Reflections & Lessons Learned

Going through GED108 was a transformative experience. Before this course, art for me was something to look at; now it’s a form of language connected to identity, history, and culture.

I learned how to read composition, understand symbolism, and appreciate how context shapes meaning. This portfolio gathers the work and reflections I completed during the course.
